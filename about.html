About me

I have been tinkering with video games since 12 years old - removing random files from the titles I had to see if the games were still working. Removing files soon became changing their contents, and soon after I started trying to make mods and custom maps for games such as The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion, S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Shadow of Chernobyl and Warcraft III: The Frozen Throne.

This soon grew into the desire to make my own projects, which is why I started learning programming since 16. In 2021 I got enrolled in University of Greenwich and moved to the United Kingdom to pursue game development degree.
