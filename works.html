All my works

Camou Adventure

A 2D sidescrolling platformer aesthetically based on Sega Genesis games that revolves around the idea of making your own paths through objectives by throwing shurikens into walls. Packed with puzzles and boss battles!

Orc Tennis

A 3 vs 3 hotseat local multiplayer game about orcs that decided to settle it in a match of tennis. Use different hit types to give the ball unique abilities, and use spells, such as dash, freezing field, knockback immunity and even raising undead, to help you win in this action-packed game.

Arctic Wasteland

The first of its kind top-down first-person shooter, this game explores the possibilities of procedural generation of the world and textures to provide unique randomly generated environments stretching for miles. Gather the resources during the day to survive the night!

Frozen Fruits

A remake of an old DOS title, Frozen Fruits is a Breakout-like puzzle game that lets you control not the platform but the ball itself! Avoid enemies, change your colour and interact with many other elements to solve all the puzzles in the game. The game includes a level editor, allowing you to create your own puzzles!
